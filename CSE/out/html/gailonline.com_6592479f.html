      Renewable Energy

      GAIL (India) Limited is committed to reduce carbon emissions and implement renewable projects. GAIL has a total installed capacity of 145 MW of alternative energy; out of which 118 MW are wind energy projects and 27 MW are solar energy projects. Read more...

      Biogas
